About the school

Mission, vision, values

We nurture confident, kind, and curious learners through a whole-child approach that balances academics, creativity, and character. Our teachers build trusting relationships, celebrate every milestone, and partner closely with families.

Our story

Founded in 2008, Pebbles Elementary grew from a small community classroom to a vibrant campus serving ages 5–12. We blend research-backed teaching with playful experiences so students feel known, challenged, and joyful.

  • Purpose-built campus with bright, airy classrooms
  • Certified teachers with ongoing professional development
  • Secure entry, health protocols, and dedicated wellbeing staff

Academics

Rigorous, playful, and personalized
